Servoo

SERVOO is a consulting and engineering company that positions itself in the development and deployment of innovative technologies in various fields and in particular those aimed at lifting African countries out of digital isolation. It was created in February 2018 and has since developed in-depth expertise through which it is gradually becoming an essential reference in solutions dedicated to foreign trade and trade facilitation (logistics platforms, one-stop-shop, Transit, Ports, Electronic payments, etc. .).

Our Mission

  • Support companies, administrations and government in the implementation of reforms based on technological innovations.

  • Implement digital services for better management of development strategies in African countries.

  • Supervise and support the development of African SMEs with a view to reducing the digital fradcture.

Our Goals

  • Breaking the digital divide between developed countries and African countries.

  • Propel a dynamic of innovation for African issues.
